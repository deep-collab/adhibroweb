Light Factory is a creatively driven production house. We're producers, directors, writers, and editors, and we've been punching above our weight since day one. Whether it's your idea or ours, we combine the 'how' with the 'why.' Bringing together our considerable in-house capability with a diverse roster of talented collaborators is the foundation of our approach. And it's the secret sauce in how we can create projects of all shapes and sizes.

LEAD CREATORS

Brett’s career choice (along with his life choices, overall) is all thanks to Batman. His childhood love for that film inspired him to make his own. He loves his job because it’s challenging and invigorating, with endless opportunities for an unbridled brainstorm. He loves his team because they depend on each other, and motivate each other. He embraces the range of emotions felt in this business, and also in the end product.

Brett Connors

Director | PARTNER

He loves his job because it’s collaborative, always evolving, and there’s no better feeling than visualizing an idea and bringing it to life. He loves his team because everyone contributes and encourages each other. It’s no surprise he loves films, but he wouldn’t dare pick a favourite because it all depends on his mood.

Clarity first

01

Everything we do connects to a larger goal for our clients, and the clearer we are on what they’re trying to accomplish, the better we can create something that makes a real impact.

Always be curious

02

We empower each other to think bigger, to get inventive, to analyze and experiment, until we uncover the best solution. If there’s a way we can elevate an idea, we’ll find it.

Motivation in motion

03

We always push ourselves to do the best possible job and enjoy every step of the way. We pride ourselves on fostering a kinetic work environment with contagious levels of passion.

Everyone has a voice

04

We champion open communication and leave egoes at the door. There’s nothing more powerful than a group of people sharing perspectives to reach a common goal together.

Reel reliable

05

Not only are we professional, and collectively represent decades of expertise, but we’re also dedicated. Nothing matters more than the success of our clients and everyone we work with.

All flavour, no fat

06

We’re a lean operation that streamlines production wherever possible. We don’t add anything extra to a project unless it contributes clear value to the final product. (*Yes, we know fat has all the flavour, but we needed the acronym to work).
